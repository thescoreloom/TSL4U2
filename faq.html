FAQ – Custom Scores

What software do you use to create scores?

All scores are created in Finale. Sibelius is not used, and Dorico may be considered in future updates.

How is a balanced arrangement defined?

Balance is determined according to the ensemble, ensuring all musical elements are clearly audible. Density, register balance, dynamics, and line readability are carefully considered.

What if I don’t have reference material?

No recordings or scores are required. Provide style, ensemble, and goals, and the score will be created accordingly.

What if the score does not meet the brief?

Consultation and score creation are included in the price and are not refundable. Minor revisions are applied if needed.

How do revisions work?

Minor revisions included:

Notation corrections, page layout, guides, doublings, octave transpositions, compatible instrument substitutions, minor key changes.

Extraordinary revisions (not included):

Stylistic or harmonic adaptations, multiple transpositions, ensemble conversion, special effects, stage modifications, reordering parts, adding sections or instruments.

Rule: substantial modifications = extra; corrections, simplifications, and doublings = included.

Payment method?

Bank transfer only, full upon delivery or deposit + balance as agreed.

How can the PDF be used?

PDF comes with timestamp and digital signature. Use is limited to the purchasing ensemble for live performance or internal education. Sharing or commercial use is prohibited.

Can I record or publish performances?

Live performance is allowed; recording or commercial release requires a separate agreement.

Types of scores provided

  • Copyrighted arrangements: exclusive license, live performance allowed, resale prohibited.
  • Public domain arrangements: educational/live use allowed, resale prohibited.
  • Original compositions: exclusive license, live performance allowed, resale prohibited.

Is the license time-limited?

The license is perpetual for the purchasing ensemble unless otherwise agreed in writing.

How are violations handled?

Unauthorized resale or distribution constitutes civil and criminal infringement under Italian Law n. 633/1941. Provider may pursue legal remedies.

What if I request major changes after delivery?

Substantial modifications (key changes, adding sections/instruments, special effects) are extra and quoted separately.

How is the final price determined?

Price is based on complexity, number of instruments, duration, and extra requests. A quote is provided before work begins.

What if the band changes instruments or ensemble?

Minor adjustments within the original ensemble are included. Substantial changes require a separate agreement.

Can the PDF be shared among musicians in the same band?

Yes, only for active members of the same ensemble. External sharing is prohibited.
